Ted Bogin

I'm a student at Dev Bootcamp, on the fast track to being a web developer. In my spare time, I write, cook, and watch Broad City. I grew up near Washington, DC. I majored in zoology at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, then moved to DC after graduating in 2013 to escape the Midwest winters, and to work in nonprofit PR. I decided I wanted a more creative, substantive, and lucrative career. So now I’m here, enjoying the journey, and the new coding skills I’m acquiring.

In my spare time, I write, read, experiment with recipes, watch TV, and grudgingly work out. I’m also the proud owner of a coloboma, an enlarged iris in my left eye that looks like a cat’s. Sadly, it doesn’t come with any special powers.
